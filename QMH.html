Quakers & Mental Health Website

Backend: Django
Frontend: Foundation
Database: Postgresql

I spent the summer of 2015 making this website alone from the ground-up. Using Django as a backend, I created a substantial administration page which will allow students and librarians to continue to add to the research and information from Special Collections on Friends Asylum. Additionally, I created the front end of the site with Foundation5 and a few visualizations with D3 and pulling from the PostgreSQL database. The goal of this site is to be a digital way to learn more about the early years of Friends Asylum, and spur further research projects.
