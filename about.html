Just having graduated from Haverford College, I've immersed myself in all aspects of web development and design. At my current job in the Haverford College Digital Scholarship Department, I've been helping to make some websites that are informational, but also have interesting features and manipulations of data. In particular, I've worked on the Quakers & Mental Health Project and created its digital platform alone from the ground up.

In addition to being fascinated by the visualizations you can make with data, I love the creative side of web development--the feeling that I'm painting a canvas that I refresh over and over and get something beautiful--and useful!
